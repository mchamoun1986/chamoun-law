About the Firm

A Legacy of Legal
Excellence in Lebanon

Founded by Maitre Elie Chamoun, Chamoun & Associates has established itself as a leading law firm in Mount Lebanon. With deep roots in the region and a thorough understanding of the Lebanese legal framework, we provide comprehensive legal solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

A graduate of the prestigious Holy Spirit University of Kaslik (USEK), Me. Chamoun brings over a decade of experience spanning civil, corporate, commercial, and dispute resolution matters. Our firm combines traditional legal values with modern, strategic approaches.
